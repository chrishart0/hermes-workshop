# WS-012 - Chattanooga outreach list

## Status

Todo

## Priority

P0

## Goal

Create a practical list of local Chattanooga organizations, communities, newsletters, schools, coworking spaces, and media outlets that might share the Hermes workshop event.

## Scope

- Research local orgs likely to reach people interested in AI, tech, entrepreneurship, professional development, Linux/open-source, students, makers, and business builders.
- Capture contact/share route where available.
- Prioritize likely-to-share channels over generic big lists.
- Produce a simple outreach table.

## Suggested columns

| Org | Audience | Why relevant | Contact/share route | Priority | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---:|---|

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] At least 15 plausible local outreach targets.
- [ ] Each target has a website or social/contact route.
- [ ] Priorities are assigned.
- [ ] Include tech/startup, university/student, coworking, maker, and local media/community categories.
- [ ] Draft a reusable short blurb they can share.

## Notes

The goal is not exhaustive research. The goal is enough good local channels to get the event in front of the right people quickly.
