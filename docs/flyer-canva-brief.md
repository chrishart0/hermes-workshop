## Core direction

Use the Hermes **landing-page marketing aesthetic**: electric blue background, pure white typography/art, classical Hermes engraving/woodcut imagery, generous negative space, and crisp event details.

Do **not** make this look like a generic AI SaaS flyer. No robot heads, neon gradients, stock matrix code, random circuit boards, or multicolor icon soup. The flyer should feel mythic, practical, and local: a striking public invitation to build something real.

## Visual layout - print flyer

Format: **US Letter portrait, 8.5 × 11 in**

Suggested structure:

1. **Top/left:** small host line: `AI Collective, Chattanooga presents`
2. **Upper left or center:** giant serif headline
3. **Right side or full background:** large cropped Hermes hero art
4. **Middle/lower left:** event details in a clean white block
5. **Small badge/pill:** `Food included`
6. **Bottom left:** QR code + registration URL
7. **Bottom right:** AI Collective logo + Hermes logo lockup

Keep the bottom logo row visually calm. The AI Collective logo is orange/black, so place both logos in a white or black footer strip if the orange clashes with Hermes blue. If Canva allows, use a white/reversed version of the AIC mark; otherwise preserve the official logo and isolate it in a small white card.

## Visual layout - social square

Format: **1080 × 1080 square**

Recommended simplified hierarchy:

- Host line at top
- Headline large
- Hermes figure cropped behind/right
- Date/time prominent
- QR code + `Register: luma.com/aic-ch-7-18`
- Logos bottom

Use less body copy than the print flyer.

## Brand style

### Colors

- Hermes blue: `#0000FF` / bright electric blue
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Optional black/near-black footer card only if needed for logo contrast

### Typography

Canva-friendly suggestions:

- Headline: **Playfair Display**, **Bodoni 72**, **Libre Baskerville**, or another high-contrast modern serif
- Body/details: **Inter**, **Manrope**, **Montserrat**, or **Aptos**
- Utility labels: **JetBrains Mono**, **Space Mono**, or another clean mono if available

### Art treatment

Use the existing Hermes hero art as the primary visual. Prefer a large dramatic crop over a small centered picture. The figure should feel monumental and slightly mythic.

If a custom/generated illustration is needed, use this prompt:

> Strict duotone event flyer artwork: vibrant electric blue background with pure white engraved line art. A classical mythological Hermes figure with winged helmet and multiple arms, rendered in detailed woodcut / etching style, holding many radiating threads of information like data streams. Sophisticated classical-meets-digital mood. Clean negative space on the left for headline text. High contrast. No gradients, no gray, no extra colors, no robot imagery.

Add all text manually in Canva for crispness.

## Print readability checks

Before printing/distributing:

- Print one black-and-white test copy; QR must still scan.
- Check readability from 6–10 feet away: headline, date, time, and location should be legible first.
- Keep QR code at least ~1.2 in wide for print; larger is safer.
- Preserve a quiet white margin around the QR code.
- Do not place QR directly over blue/illustration unless it sits inside a white square/card.

## Taste notes

Preferred final: **Make Hermes Read the Boring Stuff**. It is memorable, clear, and exactly the promise of the workshop. The supporting copy can carry the technical credibility.
