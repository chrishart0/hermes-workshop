# Flyer Variant 2 - Build Your First Useful AI Agent

## Concept

A clear, broad-appeal flyer concept for the Hermes Agent hands-on workshop. This version leads with the practical promise: attendees will leave with their first useful AI agent, not just a chatbot demo.

## Primary public copy

**Host line**
AI Collective, Chattanooga presents

**Headline**
BUILD YOUR FIRST
USEFUL AI AGENT

**Subhead**
A hands-on Hermes Agent workshop for people who want AI to actually do useful work.

**Body copy**
Install Hermes, connect a model, learn the agent workflow, and build a practical starter agent you can keep improving after the workshop.

**Event block**
Saturday, July 18
1:00 PM – 5:00 PM EDT
Business Development Center
100 Cherokee Blvd., Chattanooga

**Utility line**
Food included. Bring a laptop.

**CTA**
Register at luma.com/aic-ch-7-18
Scan the QR code to reserve your spot.

## Visual direction

Use the Hermes landing-page aesthetic:

- Electric blue background: `#0000FF`
- Pure white typography and art: `#FFFFFF`
- High-contrast serif headline, ideally Playfair Display, Bodoni 72, or Libre Baskerville
- Clean sans-serif details, ideally Inter, Manrope, Montserrat, or Aptos
- Classical engraved / woodcut Hermes line art
- Spacious layout with a strong left-side copy area and visual energy on the right
- No robot imagery, no stock circuit boards, no generic SaaS gradients, no multicolor icon clutter

## Canva layout recommendation

### Print flyer - US Letter portrait

1. Top-left: `AI Collective, Chattanooga presents`
2. Main left column: large serif headline `BUILD YOUR FIRST / USEFUL AI AGENT`
3. Under headline: one-sentence subhead and short body copy
4. Lower-left or lower-center: date, time, venue, address
5. Bottom-left: QR code in a quiet white card with `luma.com/aic-ch-7-18`
6. Bottom-right: AI Collective logo + Hermes logo lockup, isolated in a calm footer/card if needed for contrast

### Social square - 1080 × 1080

Use the same concept with less copy:

- Host line
- Big headline
- Date/time and venue
- `Food included. Bring a laptop.`
- QR code + registration URL
- Logos along the bottom

## Print/readability checks

- Keep the headline, date, time, and location readable from 6–10 feet away.
- Keep the QR code at least about 1.2 inches wide for print and preserve a white quiet zone around it.
- Do not place the QR code directly over blue artwork unless it sits inside a white card.
- Print one quick test copy and confirm the QR scans before public distribution.
