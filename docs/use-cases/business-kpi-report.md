## Who it helps

Founders, operators, managers, and small teams that need a daily pulse without dashboard hopping.

## What it watches

Revenue exports, order data, refund counts, support queues, product analytics, and customer feedback.

## What it knows about you

The business model, normal ranges, current launches, known issues, and what deserves interruption.

## What it produces

A short daily report: what moved, why it might matter, and what needs a human decision.

## First safe version

Use copied exports or read-only replicas. Start with one metric source and one support source. No customer PII in prompts.

## Stretch version

Let the agent draft follow-up questions, team updates, or customer-response drafts for human review.
