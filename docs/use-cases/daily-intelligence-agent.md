## Who it helps

Anyone who starts the day by checking newsletters, feeds, releases, dashboards, or local events.

## What it watches

Newsletters, release notes, advisories, websites, feeds, dashboards, calendars, or event listings.

## What it knows about you

Your projects, stack, city, interests, current commitments, and what counts as noise.

## What it produces

A short report ranked by relevance, with sources and a feedback loop.

## First safe version

Use the workshop Daily Intelligence setup guide, keep it read-only, run one report, then give feedback so Hermes updates the skill.

## Stretch version

Schedule it for daily delivery through Discord, Telegram, Slack, email, or a PDF.
