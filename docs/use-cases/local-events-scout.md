## Who it helps

Community builders, professionals, hobbyists, families, and anyone trying to be more intentional locally.

## What it watches

Venue calendars, meetup pages, community newsletters, local news, LinkedIn events, and city pages.

## What it knows about you

Your city, interests, schedule, distance limits, budget, and venues you like or dislike.

## What it produces

A short list of events worth considering, with why each one matches.

## First safe version

Start with three local sources and one week of events. Require links and drop generic recurring filler.

## Stretch version

Send a Thursday or Friday weekend brief and learn from what you actually attended.
