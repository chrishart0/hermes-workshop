Track events, appointments, renewals, reminders, purchases, travel, and recurring chores.

## Who it helps

Anyone with scattered life logistics they keep re-checking manually.

## What it watches

Calendar exports, email copies, notes, receipts, travel confirmations, event pages, and task lists.

## What it knows about you

Your location, preferences, important dates, budget boundaries, and what should never be booked automatically.

## What it produces

A weekly admin brief with upcoming deadlines, missing information, and suggested next actions.

## First safe version

Start read-only with copied inputs. Let it remind and draft, not purchase, book, or cancel.

## Stretch version

Connect a delivery channel and add explicit approval flows for narrow actions.
