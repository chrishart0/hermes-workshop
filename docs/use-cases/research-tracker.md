# Research Tracking Agent

Watch a topic, grant, policy, market, company, competitor, or project for meaningful changes.

## Who it helps

Researchers, writers, grant seekers, analysts, founders, and policy watchers.

## What it watches

Websites, RSS feeds, search results, newsletters, public filings, grant pages, repos, or docs.

## What it knows about you

Your research question, inclusion criteria, trusted sources, competitors, deadlines, and stale claims.

## What it produces

A change report that says what changed, why it matters, and where the evidence is.

## First safe version

Pick one narrow topic and three sources. Require source links and a section for negative searches.

## Stretch version

Maintain a living brief, bibliography, or source-backed claims file.
