# Support and Feedback Summarizer

Read support or feedback exports and find repeated pain, urgency, and product signals.

## Who it helps

Product managers, founders, support leads, and solo builders with scattered customer feedback.

## What it watches

Support exports, feedback forms, app reviews, Discord threads, GitHub issues, or sales notes.

## What it knows about you

Current product priorities, known bugs, important customers, and categories to ignore.

## What it produces

Themes, urgent threads, repeated objections, and suggested next actions.

## First safe version

Start with an anonymized export or copied sample. Require citations back to ticket IDs or row numbers.

## Stretch version

Generate weekly product-priority memos or draft customer follow-ups for human approval.
