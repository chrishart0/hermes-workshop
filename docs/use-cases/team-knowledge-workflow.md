## Who it helps

Operators, project managers, recruiters, sales teams, and professionals with recurring browser/file workflows.

## What it watches

Web apps, docs, spreadsheets, internal exports, inboxes, forms, and recurring checklists.

## What it knows about you

The process steps, decision rules, review boundaries, and examples of good output.

## What it produces

A completed draft, checklist, report, or handoff package for human review.

## First safe version

Pick one narrow workflow. Let Hermes read, summarize, and draft first. Do not let it submit public forms or send messages until reviewed.

## Stretch version

Add browser or computer-use automation for repetitive steps after the written workflow is reliable.
