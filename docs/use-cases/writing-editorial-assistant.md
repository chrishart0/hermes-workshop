## Who it helps

Writers, marketers, educators, analysts, and founders who produce recurring content.

## What it watches

Notes, source docs, transcripts, newsletters, research folders, outlines, and prior drafts.

## What it knows about you

Audience, voice, claims that need evidence, forbidden claims, and publishing standards.

## What it produces

A brief, outline, draft, source checklist, or edit pass focused on the next publishing step.

## First safe version

Point it at one source folder and one output format. Require citations for factual claims.

## Stretch version

Schedule a weekly content brief or have it maintain a source-backed article backlog.
